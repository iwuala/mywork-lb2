Apple Pile

This was my groundmothers apple pie recipe. i have never seen another one
quite like it. it will always be favourite and has won me several first place
prizes in local competitions. i hope it becomes one of your favourites as well!

Ingredients

  • -1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch double crust pie
  • -1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • -3 table spoon all- purpose flour
  • -1/4 cup of water
  • -3 table spoon all-purpose flour
  • -1/4 cup of water
  • -1/2 cup of white sugar
  • -1/2 cup of packed brown sugar
  • -8 granny smith apples - peeled, cored and sliced

Directions

1) Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Melt the butter in a saucepan. stir in
flour to form a paste. add water, white sugar and brown sugar, and bring to a boil. reduce
temperature and let simmer.

2) Place the bottom crust in your pan. Fill with apples, mounded slightly. Cover a
lattice work crust. Gently pour the sugar and butter liquid over the crust. Pour slowly so
that it does not run off.

3) Bake 15 minutes in the preheated oven. Reduce the tempertature to 350 degrees F (175
degrees C). Continue baking for 35 to 45 minutes, until apples are soft
